This Privacy Policy explains what information the MirrorCTT Chrome extension may access, why it is needed, how it is handled, and the choices available to users.

1. Overview

MirrorCTT is a productivity Chrome extension intended for authorized call-center and lead-booking personnel. It works with supported customer relationship management, dialer, and lead-management webpages to organize information already available to the signed-in user and assist with lead, callback, appointment, and booking workflows.

This policy applies to the MirrorCTT Chrome extension. It does not apply to websites or services operated by others, including websites on which MirrorCTT may run.

2. Information MirrorCTT Accesses

To provide its core functionality, MirrorCTT may access webpage content that is visible or available within supported websites. Depending on the page, this content may include:

  • customer names, phone numbers, email addresses, and addresses;
  • lead IDs and lead, project, or account information;
  • client or campaign names;
  • appointment, callback, and booking details;
  • call dispositions and notes displayed on supported pages; and
  • similar workflow information displayed in a supported browser tab.

MirrorCTT is intended to access only the information needed to provide its stated, user-facing functionality. The exact information available to the extension depends on the supported website and the page the user is viewing.

3. How Information Is Used

MirrorCTT uses accessed information only to operate, maintain, and improve the extension’s user-facing features. This may include reading supported page content, organizing relevant details, and displaying those details or related assistance to the user.

MirrorCTT does not use accessed information for advertising, personalized advertising, or the creation of advertising profiles.

4. Local Storage

MirrorCTT may store extension settings, preferences, and user-saved workflow information locally in the user’s browser. Depending on the features used, locally stored information may include saved lead details, callback information, booking information, customer names, phone numbers, addresses, project details, and related notes selected or saved by the user.

This information is stored locally to preserve the user’s workflow between browser sessions and is not transmitted to the developer unless expressly stated elsewhere in this policy.

Locally stored extension data remains on the user’s device unless the browser syncs it under the user’s Chrome settings.

5. Data Transmission

MirrorCTT does not transmit webpage content accessed from supported tabs to developer-controlled servers or unrelated third-party services. Processing of that content occurs locally within the user’s browser.

Important: Standard network requests made by the websites a user visits are controlled by those websites and are outside MirrorCTT’s operation.

6. Data Sharing and Sale

MirrorCTT does not sell user data or share information accessed from supported webpages with unrelated third parties. Because locally processed information is not received or controlled by the developer, the developer generally cannot access or disclose that information.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

MirrorCTT retains locally stored settings and preferences for as long as the extension remains installed or until the user clears the extension’s data. Users can remove locally stored data by clearing MirrorCTT’s site or extension data in Chrome, resetting available settings within the extension, or uninstalling the extension.

Users may contact the developer at stephensadam9@gmail.com with questions or requests concerning privacy or deletion.

8. Data Security

MirrorCTT is designed to limit access to information needed for its functionality and to rely on Chrome’s extension security controls. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

No method of storage or electronic processing is completely secure. Accordingly, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

9. Chrome Web Store Limited Use Disclosure

MirrorCTT’s use of information obtained through Chrome extension APIs complies with the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including its Limited Use requirements.

Information received through Chrome APIs is used only to provide or improve MirrorCTT’s prominent, user-facing features. It is not used for personalized advertising, sold to third parties, or used to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

10. Children’s Privacy

MirrorCTT is not directed to children under 13, and the developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through the extension. If you believe a child has provided personal information through MirrorCTT, contact the developer so the concern can be reviewed and appropriate action taken.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to MirrorCTT, legal requirements, or privacy practices. When the policy changes, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised. Material changes will be communicated through an appropriate channel when required by law.
